Radhey Kitchen and Chai Bar

  • Written by Libby on 29.06.2015
  • 2 Comments
  • TAGS: Exploring, Fitzroy

Today I went to Fitzroy again and had a lovely late lunch. I'd spent the morning at the library finishing off CodeSchool's Bootstrap course. See, check out this sweet blog site I've made!

After completing the course I was starving so I wandered through the neat alleyways to Bourke St and caught Tram 96 to Nicholson and walked to Brunswick St. I spent 10 minutes walking up and then down the street, finally settling on a small cafe - Radhey Kitchen & Chai Bar.

"Radhey has a rustic cafe-style menu which is influenced by Mediterranean, Asian and Indian flavours. These delicious home-style creations are brought to you by our awesome team in the kitchen. Being mindful of dietary requirements, the kitchen crew put heart and soul into every tasty meal they create."

I ordered a Mediterranean Foccocia and an Iced Chocolate, and I sat in the window watching people walk past.
